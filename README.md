Services We Offer
We have developed a multifaceted system to meet your diverse needs:
Financial Services & Loans: We offer Pi loans with affordable terms (10% Interest and 112% Bond/Collateral) alongside secure Escrow services to protect your transactions.
Bill Payments: Easily pay for LUKU (Electricity), Water bills (DAWASA), DSTV, StarTimes, and purchase mobile data bundles for all networks (Vodacom, Tigo, Airtel, Halotel).
Government & Employment Services: We assist with Passport applications, NSSF benefit claims, and account registration on the Ajira Portal.
Document Processing: Professional services for converting PDF to Word, Hardcopy to Softcopy, Media to Word, as well as general Typing and Editing services.
Education & Business: Crypto trading training, setup of Instagram and TikTok business accounts, and entrepreneurship support (Agency Registration).
Entertainment: A dedicated gaming and betting platform via BET WILLIAM.PI.
